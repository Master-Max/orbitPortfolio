CSDNet

There are many people who are good at breaking technology... - Every IT Admin Ever.

This was a great place to work, especially for someone who was a full time student the rest of the year. The position was called Summer IT Technitian, and it was here that I first worked with computers professionally.

As the youngest of my collegues at the time I did a lot of grunt work. Desktop computers of the day while no longer the size of a room, still ammounted to 40 pounds per unit.
